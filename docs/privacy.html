Scanova

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Overview

Scanova respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the app accesses and how it is used.

Permissions We Use

  • Camera access, to scan paper documents into the app
  • Photo Library add access, to save exported document pages as images to your Photos library

How Data Is Handled

Your documents are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Scanova may process document text on-device to support OCR, smart naming, and document understanding features.

App preferences and small settings may be stored locally on your device to remember your experience.

Subscription status is handled through Apple’s in-app purchase system.

Scanova does not use third-party advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking for advertising.

Subscriptions

Auto-renewable subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple. You can restore purchases inside the app and manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.
